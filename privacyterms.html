PRIVACY POLICY

This privacy policy sets out how Lozupone & Pace uses and protects any information that you voluntarily provide to Lozupone & Pace when you use our apps. Lozupone & Pace is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. Lozupone & Pace may change this policy from time to time by updating this page. You should check this page from time to time. This policy was updated and is effective as of 24 February 2023.

App Privacy

Downloading our apps does not require any registration of personal information with Lozupone & Pace. Lozupone & Pace does not collect any personally-identifying data on you or your use of any of our applications. However, Lozupone & Pace may make use of non-personally-identifying usage statistics (i.e., clicks, sensor mode usage, time in app, etc.). Lozupone & Pace does not access a user's sensor data of any type. After installation, any physical data measurements recorded using the mobile device's sensors is stored in the internal memory of the mobile device, unless otherwise transmitted voluntarily by the user to a third party through the data export feature. Lozupone & Pace apps do not require Internet permissions.

App Permissions Explained

Each Lozupone & Pace app requires different levels of permissions depending upon the sensors capabilities of each app. At most, the following permissions are required for the following purposes: Photos/Media/Files to save and/or read recorded sensor data and Storage to save and/or read recorded sensor data
